About Us

Java House is a cozy coffee shop that was established in 2005. Our mission is to provide our customers with high-quality coffee and excellent customer service. We are proud to use only the finest beans from around the world and our baristas are trained to create the perfect cup of coffee every time.

We value sustainability and fair trade practices, and strive to be a responsible member of our local community. Our shop has a warm and inviting atmosphere, with comfortable seating and free Wi-Fi. Come visit us today and experience the best coffee in town!
